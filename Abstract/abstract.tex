\begin{abstract}

    I study approval-based elections, where preferences are in the 1D-Euclidean domain.
    I explore multiple areas, notably: 
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item I create domain detection algorithms and evaluate the results.
        \item I look for efficient method of computing election results.
    \end{enumerate}
    For the first issue I find statistical relation with other well known domains.
    For the second one I create polynomial algorithm and compare it with LP solutions. (WIP)
