In this section I explore VCR domain and describe found properties.

\section{(WIP) Experiment description - search of domain}
Experiments can be grouped into 2 categories. First search of small profiles, where I check every combination, 
and second one for larger profiles where I generate random preferences.
\newline

\noindent Algorithm steps:
\begin{enumerate}\label{SearchProfilesAlgo}
    \item\label{SearchGenerationStep} Generate approval sets of the profiles for given C and V elections.
    \item Filter out profiles not fulfilling \textit{VCR} property. Described in the section \ref{VCR Detection}
    \item Group profiles by \textit{VR, CR} and \textit{VCR not VR nor CR} properties (\textit{VCR !COP})
    \item Aggregate statistics
\end{enumerate}
